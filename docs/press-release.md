# RoomLight — Smarter Lighting for Modern Hotels

**March 2026** – RoomLight introduces a new way for hotels to manage room lighting intelligently while offering guests a personalized and comfortable stay.

## The Challenge

Hotels today face increasing pressure to reduce energy consumption while maintaining a high-quality guest experience. Traditional lighting systems are often static, inefficient, and difficult to manage consistently across multiple rooms or properties.

At the same time, guests expect more control and comfort during their stay.

## The RoomLight Approach

RoomLight rethinks hotel room lighting as a smart, adaptable system.

For hotels, RoomLight enables centralized control and energy-aware optimization across rooms. Lighting settings can be aligned with brand standards and adjusted efficiently when needed.

For guests, RoomLight provides simple and intuitive ways to personalize room lighting — creating the right atmosphere for relaxing, working, or sleeping.

By combining sustainability and comfort, RoomLight helps hotels reduce waste without compromising the guest experience.

## Our Vision

"We believe lighting should support both operational efficiency and memorable stays," said the RoomLight team. "Hotels shouldn't have to choose between sustainability and atmosphere."

## Getting Started

RoomLight is designed to integrate smoothly into hotel environments and scale from individual properties to entire hotel chains. RoomLight consists of a secure cloud-based management dashboard for hotel operators and a guest interface available via in-room remote or mobile app.
